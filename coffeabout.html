History

Ayngaran Coffee (Kumbakonam Filter Coffee) was
originally founded by entrepreneure Mr.Ramachandran
and Mr.Srinivasan. The first outlet was launched
in Kumbakonam at Utchipillayar Kovil.
Ayngaran Coffee has since witnessed the launch another
between Sarangapanai Temple, Utchipillayar Kovil, Ramasamy
Temple and Athi Kumbeswarar Temple.

Vision

We aim to serve the best Kumbakonam
filter coffee to all coffee lovers. We
strive to prove that having a cup of coffee
with friends and family shouldn't be pretentious
with unreasonable prices, fancy decor and mediocre snacks.
To that end, we have established Ayngaran Coffee in two
places like Utchipillaiyar Kovil and Opposite to Vasan
eye care, Kumbakonam.
